Overview

Properties is a new generation, Nellore-based, real-estate development company. We create functional, user-centric spaces that are environmentally sustainable using materials of the highest quality and engineering excellence. Multiversal Infra Private Limited in delivering residential and commercial projects is built on the core values that are embedded in us from the blueprint to brick. With the unwavering confidence in the mantra that ‘only good quality and great design stands the test of time’, we continue to deliver to hundreds of happy customers.

  • To be most customer centric company
  • To make buying/construction of your dream property as simple and transparent as buying products online

Mission

To rise above status quo by creating best-in-class living and working spaces that are contemporary in design, ergonomic, environmentally sustainable and constructed using materials of the highest quality and engineering excellence with a commitment to timelines

Vision

To be a brand that is admired as an industry leader for its thoughtfully designed, high quality, innovative life-spaces, is recognized for its customer and people practices, We are in pursuit of Customer Delight through Excellence in product, highest standards in Quality
